The algebraic solution to the double pendulum

The configuration space is an algebraic surface in \(\mathbb{R}^4\) : $$\left\{(a,b,c,d)\in \mathbb{R}^4|a^2+b^2=1\land (c-a)^2+(d-b)^2=1\right\}$$

One parametrization based on the stereographic projection is :

$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} \frac{a \left(1-\lambda ^2\right)}{\lambda ^2+1}-\frac{2 b \lambda }{\lambda ^2+1} & \frac{2 a \lambda }{\lambda ^2+1}+\frac{b \left(1-\lambda ^2\right)}{\lambda ^2+1} \\ \frac{a \left(1-\lambda ^2\right)}{\lambda ^2+1}-\frac{2 b \lambda }{\lambda ^2+1}+\frac{c \left(1-\mu ^2\right)}{\mu ^2+1}-\frac{2 d \mu }{\mu ^2+1} & \frac{2 a \lambda }{\lambda ^2+1}+\frac{b \left(1-\lambda ^2\right)}{\lambda ^2+1}+\frac{2 c \mu }{\mu ^2+1}+\frac{d \left(1-\mu ^2\right)}{\mu ^2+1} \\ \end{array} \right) $$

Viewing a long sequence

The textarea above can not quite be used for very long DNA sequences. To do so you may first download it as a file in FASTA format:

Lots of nucleotid sequences from actual biological data are available for free on various websites, such as USA's National Center for Biotechnology Information. On this particular website, you may select the "nucleotide" database, enter keywords such as "homo sapiens chromosome 7", click on FASTA and then in the "send" menu, pick the "file" destination.
